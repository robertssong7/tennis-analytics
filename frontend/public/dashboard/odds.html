We're building a real-time odds comparison tool that layers our model probabilities (Brier 0.1807) against live bookmaker lines to surface edge opportunities.

What's being built

The odds module will connect to tennis-data.co.uk historical odds data and live bookmaker feeds to create a unified view of model probability vs. implied probability. When our ensemble produces a win probability significantly above the implied odds, that's a positive expected value situation.

Our ensemble achieves a Brier score of 0.1807 on a zero-leakage 2024 temporal hold-out, comparable to leading market models. The goal is to identify calibrated edge, not to encourage gambling.
