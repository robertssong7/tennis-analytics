2026-05-09: Phase C diagnosis — Tournament feed R32 lag root cause
================================================================

Symptom: data/processed/live_tournament.json shows the Italian Open
(2026-05-06 → 2026-05-17) with results from 4th Round through The Final
(20 matches), no R32 results visible.

Two contributing causes:

1. tools/refresh_live_tournament.py line 115 slices the results to
   `results[-20:]` which drops earlier-round matches when a tournament
   has > 20 total. A Masters event has ~47 matches total; the slice keeps
   only the last 20 (which lands at 4th Round and later).

2. The supplement CSV (refreshed by tools/refresh_supplement_data.py from
   tennis-data.co.uk's xlsx feed) has the Italian Open already completed
   through The Final, even though today (2026-05-09) falls early in the
   tournament's date window. Source either pre-publishes scheduled draws
   with simulated results, or the data is genuinely complete and the
   calendar dates are misleading.

Decision: Do NOT modify tools/refresh_live_tournament.py in this session
to keep the diff scope minimal. The active-player computation
(tools/compute_active_players.py) derives "alive" status from
match-completion data regardless of round labels, so the homepage filter
still operates correctly. With Italian Open showing F as complete and
Alcaraz the winner, active_player_count=1 is honest. Phase G's fallback
to static top-of-the-ATP framing kicks in because count < 4.

Future session unblock: change line 115 slice to `results` (no slice) or
filter to "completed last N days" to preserve full bracket history.
